COOKIES

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Nifty Wealth Solutions as Research Analyst uses cookies to help and identify and track visitors, their usage of the website, and their website access preferences. Visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Nifty Wealth Solutions websites, with the drawback that certain features of our websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
